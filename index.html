Week 1: Foundations & Group Formation

Explore core principles of group dynamics through mythic archetypes, Norse creation myths, and Bion's double-bind. Learn fundamental concepts through real-world examples.

Week 2: Power Dynamics & Leadership

Examine leadership through mythodynamic hierarchies and group projections. Study succession patterns through archetypal narratives and case studies. Exploring underground authority networks and group alliances.

Week 3: Conflict & Attraction

Analyze group tensions and strategies to navigate discord. Exploring common threads between psychological polarities.

Week 4: Group Defense Mechanisms

Understand scapegoating patterns, groupthink, and boundary-setting through archetypal narratives.

Week 5: Sabotage & The Crisis Compulsion

Explore The Crisis Compulsion, shadow integration, and group transformation. Learn how groups evolve through crisis using archetypal narratives and real-world examples.

Week 6: Integration & Application

Synthesize archetypal narratives and build practical leadership awareness. Create action plans for applying course concepts to real-world group challenges.
